Gnocchi with peas, pesto and spinach

Description

Check out this quick, creamy gnocchi recipe with fresh pesto, bright peas and baby spinach – ready in just 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • baby spinach 50g, chopped
  • frozen peas 100g, defrosted
  • fresh pesto 4 tbsp
  • crème fraîche 3 tbsp
  • gnocchi 300g pack
  • pea shoots to serve (optional)

Steps

  1. Put the spinach and peas in a non-stick pan with some seasoning and a splash of water, and heat, stirring, until the spinach has wilted.
  2. Add the pesto and crème fraîche, and gently heat through.
  3. Cook the gnocchi in a pan of boiling water following pack instructions then scoop out with a slotted spoon into the spinach pan.
  4. Toss everything together, then serve in warmed bowls topped with a few pea shoots, if using.
