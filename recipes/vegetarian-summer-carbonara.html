Vegetarian carbonara

Description

Give pesto pasta a twist with this quick and easy recipe for two. A simple homemade pesto with rocket and parmesan is the perfect sauce for tagliatelle

  • rocket 75g, washed
  • garlic 1 clove
  • pine nuts 25g
  • olive oil
  • parmesan (or vegetarian alternative) 25g, grated
  • tagliatelle 150g
  • peas 150g

Steps

  1. Put the rocket, garlic and pine nuts in a small food processor and whizz, adding 4 tbsp olive oil as you go (add extra if you prefer a thinner texture). Stir in the parmesan and season.
  2. Cook the tagliatelle adding the peas for the last minute of cooking time.
  3. Drain, keeping 1 tbsp of the cooking water. Toss the pesto, pasta, peas and water together and serve with extra cheese if you like.
